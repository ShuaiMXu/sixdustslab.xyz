For PRC deployments, also complete the PIPIA fields in Section 8.

7 Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment (FRIA) — EU AI Act Art. 27

ℹ️ When to Complete This Section
Complete Section 7 only for EU Annex III high-risk use cases (employment screening, creditworthiness, insurance pricing, public benefit eligibility, essential services access) from 2 August 2026. This FRIA may be integrated into the DPIA as a combined document. Public-body deployers and Annex III §5 deployers must complete and make available on request.

8 PI Protection Impact Assessment (PIPIA) — PIPL Arts. 55–56

📌 When to Complete This Section
Complete Section 8 for PRC deployments involving: sensitive personal information; automated decision-making with major impact; entrusted processing; cross-border data transfers; public disclosure of PI; or processing of minors' PI. Retain PIPIA for minimum 3 years.
