What does "Gibraltar-regulated" actually mean for my business?
It means your AI service provider operates under a mature, GDPR-equivalent legal framework. Gibraltar has a 2004 Data Protection Act substantially aligned to EU GDPR. From July 2026, EU↔Gibraltar data flows freely under the UK-EU Treaty on Gibraltar — no Standard Contractual Clauses required. For UK clients, Gibraltar is already adequate. This gives you a European-grade regulatory foundation without the cost and bureaucracy of an EU establishment.
"直布罗陀监管"对我的业务实际意味着什么？
这意味着您的 AI 服务提供商在成熟的、与 GDPR 等同的法律框架下运营。直布罗陀 2004 年《数据保护法》与 EU GDPR 实质对齐。2026 年 7 月起，根据《英国-欧盟直布罗陀条约》，欧盟↔直布罗陀数据可自由流转，无需标准合同条款。对于英国客户，直布罗陀已被视为充分性地区。这为您提供了欧洲级别的监管基础，而无需欧盟设立机构的成本和官僚程序。
O que significa "regulado em Gibraltar" para o meu negócio?
Significa que o seu fornecedor de IA opera sob um quadro jurídico maduro, equivalente ao RGPD. A partir de Julho de 2026, os fluxos de dados UE↔Gibraltar são livres ao abrigo do Tratado Gibraltar-UE — sem CCPs necessárias. Para clientes do RU, Gibraltar já é considerado adequado. Oferece uma base regulatória europeia sem o custo de estabelecimento na UE.
Do I need the DORA Enterprise contract?
Only if you are an EU financial entity (bank, insurance company, investment firm, payment institution, crypto-asset service provider, etc.) subject to Regulation (EU) 2022/2554, AND you use the Service in connection with critical or important functions. If you're not sure, contact us — we'll assess free of charge. Most businesses do not need DORA. The standard service agreement explicitly states it is a non-DORA contract.
我需要DORA企业合同吗？
只有当您是须遵守《欧盟数字运营韧性法》(DORA, Regulation (EU) 2022/2554)的欧盟金融实体（银行、保险公司、投资公司、支付机构、加密资产服务提供商等），且将本服务用于关键或重要职能时才需要。如不确定，请联系我们——免费评估。大多数企业不需要DORA。标准服务协议明确声明其为非DORA合同。
Preciso do contrato DORA Empresarial?
Apenas se for uma entidade financeira UE sujeita ao Regulamento (UE) 2022/2554 E utilizar o Serviço em funções críticas ou importantes. Se não tiver a certeza, contacte-nos — avaliação gratuita. A maioria das empresas não precisa de DORA. O contrato padrão declara explicitamente que não é um contrato DORA.
What happens if Claude Max changes price or Anthropic changes their terms?
This is exactly why we separate the AI account cost from the platform fee. If Anthropic changes pricing, that change affects your direct relationship with Anthropic, not your SDL-HappyClaw contract. For Business All-In where we manage the AI account, we commit to giving you 30 days' notice of any upstream price change and the right to terminate without penalty if you don't accept it. We always pass changes through at zero markup. And if Anthropic ever changes terms, your AI relationship is yours, not locked into us.
如果Claude Max价格变动或Anthropic修改条款怎么办？
这正是我们将AI账户费用与平台费分开的原因。如果Anthropic调整定价，变化影响您与Anthropic的直接关系，而非您的SDL-HappyClaw合同。对于我们管理AI账户的Business All-In方案，我们承诺提前30天通知任何上游价格变动，并赋予您免责终止权（如不接受变动）。我们始终零加价传递变动。如果Anthropic修改条款，您与AI的关系归您所有，不会被我们锁定。
